Are treatments painful? +
Most treatments cause minimal discomfort. We use premium topical anaesthetics and ultra-fine needles to ensure your experience is as comfortable as possible. Dr. Gilardi will discuss every step with you before beginning.
How long do results last? +
Results vary by treatment. Anti-wrinkle injections typically last 3–4 months, dermal fillers 12–18 months, and skin rejuvenation treatments provide cumulative long-term benefits. Dr. Gilardi will give you a realistic timeline during your consultation.
Is there any downtime? +
Most treatments have minimal downtime. Some patients experience mild redness or swelling for 24–48 hours. Dr. Gilardi provides detailed aftercare instructions to minimise any side effects and optimise your results.
How soon can I return to work? +
The majority of our patients return to work the same day or the following day. We recommend avoiding strenuous exercise and direct heat for 24 hours post-treatment. Your aftercare plan will include specific guidance for your treatment.
How do I book a consultation? +
You can book your consultation using the form below, by calling us on +44 20 7123 4567, or by emailing hello@jessicaroseaesthetics.com. We aim to respond to all enquiries within one working day.
